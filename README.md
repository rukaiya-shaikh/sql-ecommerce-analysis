# E-commerce Sales Performance Analysis (SQL)

## Problem Statement
A retail company wants to analyze its sales data to understand overall revenue, profit, customer performance, and loss-making orders.

## Dataset
Source: Superstore sales dataset  
Rows: 5000  
Columns: 15  

## Tools Used
- SQLite
- SQL
- Google Drive

## Key Business Questions
1. What are the total sales and total profit?
2. Which product categories generate the most revenue?
3. Which regions perform best in sales?
4. Who are the top 10 customers?
5. Are there any loss-making orders?
6. What is the monthly sales trend?

## Key Insights
- Total Sales: 12506815.51
- Total Profit:  2239711.13
- Top Category by Sales: Office Supplies
- Top Region by Sales: South
- Number of Loss-Making Orders: 474
- Profit Margin: 17.91 %

## Files in this Project

## Conclusion
This analysis helps the business understand revenue drivers, identify top customers and products, and detect loss areas for better decision-making.
